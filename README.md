# retail-store-excel-analysis

## Objective
To analyze retail store sales data and identify sale performance using excel.

## Tools used
- Microsoft Excel

## Dataset Overview
- Customer name and city
- Product and category
- Payment mode
- Quantity sold and revenue

## Key Analysis Performed
- Identification of high and low performing products
- Most used payment mode
- City wise and Product wise revenue comparision

## Key Insights
- Notebook is the highest selling product
- Bangalore generates highest revenue
- UPI is most ued payment mode
- Top 2 customers contribute major revenue

## Conclusion
This Excel based analysis helps to understand the areas which needs improvisation and attention and supports business decissions.
